3 Day Female Workout Plan
(No Chest / No Hip Thrusts)

Focus on glutes, legs, back, arms, and core. Train Monday, Wednesday, and Friday for optimal results.

Monday – Lower Body (Glutes & Hamstrings)

  • Romanian Deadlifts (4x10-12)
  • Bulgarian Split Squats (3x10 each leg)
  • Glute Kickbacks (3x15 each leg)
  • Hamstring Curls (3x12)
  • Reverse Lunges (3x12 each leg)
  • Standing Calf Raises (3x15-20)
  • Plank (3x45 sec)

Wednesday – Upper Body (Back, Shoulders, Arms, Core)

  • Lat Pulldowns (4x10)
  • Seated Cable Row (3x12)
  • Face Pulls (3x15)
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises (3x12)
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls (3x12)
  • Tricep Rope Pushdowns (3x12)
  • Hanging Leg Raises (3x12-15)

Friday – Lower Body (Quads & Glutes)

  • Barbell Back Squats (4x10)
  • Leg Press (3x12)
  • Walking Lunges (3x12 each leg)
  • Step-Ups (3x12 each leg)
  • Abductor Machine (3x15)
  • Standing Calf Raises (3x15-20)
  • Russian Twists (3x20 total)
